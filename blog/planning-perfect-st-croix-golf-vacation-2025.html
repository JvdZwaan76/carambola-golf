Travel Documentation and Entry Requirements

As a US territory, St. Croix offers streamlined entry for US citizens and clear requirements for international visitors. Understanding the documentation needed ensures a smooth start to your golf vacation.

For US Citizens

US citizens need only a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license or passport) for entry to St. Croix. However, carrying a passport is recommended for easier re-entry to the mainland US and international travel connections.

For International Visitors

Non-US citizens must present a valid passport and any required visas for US entry. The same immigration requirements that apply to mainland US travel apply to the US Virgin Islands. Check the official USVI tourism website for current entry requirements.

Travel Tip: Shipping Your Golf Clubs for a Hassle-Free Vacation

If you prefer playing with your own clubs rather than renting, shipping them ahead is a smart move for your St. Croix tripâavoiding airline baggage fees and ensuring they're ready at Carambola upon arrival. In 2025, options abound for safe, cost-effective shipping.

Best Ways to Ship

Use specialized services like Ship Sticks (door-to-door, white-glove service; 3.5M+ clubs shipped, rated best by Golf Digest) or Luggage Forward for international/domestic ease. For DIY, carriers like FedEx (Ground for 1-5 days) or UPS (pack at stores) are reliable. Alternatives include ShipGo (starting $44.99) or LugLess for affordable options.

TSA Guidelines for Golf Equipment

If you choose to travel with your clubs, review the TSA golf equipment guidelines. Golf clubs are allowed in checked baggage but not carry-on. Pack them in a hard case and remove all loose items from golf bags.

Costs in 2025

Expect $25-34 for one club or $123-137 for a full set domestically (varies by weight/distance). FedEx domestic averages $100 each way; specialized services like Ship Sticks may cost more ($50-150) but include insurance and tracking. Avoid peak seasons for lower rates.

Tips for Success

  • Pack clubs in a hard case with padding
  • Keep weight under 50 lbs to avoid fees
  • Ship 3-5 days early for timely arrival
  • Consider insurance ($1-2 per $100 value)
