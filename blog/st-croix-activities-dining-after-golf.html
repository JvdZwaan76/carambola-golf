What to Do After Your Round at Carambola Golf Resort: Fine Dining and Activities in St. Croix

Discover the best restaurants, cultural sites, and activities to complement your golf experience at Carambola with our curated local guide to St. Croix's finest offerings.

After conquering Carambola Golf Club's challenging Robert Trent Jones Sr. design, the adventure is far from over. St. Croix offers a rich tapestry of experiences that perfectly complement your golf vacation—from world-class dining and historic tours to pristine beaches and vibrant cultural attractions. Whether you're seeking relaxation, adventure, or a taste of local culture, the island provides the perfect backdrop for unforgettable post-golf experiences.

This comprehensive guide reveals the best restaurants, activities, and hidden gems that will elevate your Carambola golf vacation from great to extraordinary. From waterfront dining with Caribbean flavors to snorkeling adventures in crystal-clear waters, discover how to make the most of your time on this beautiful US Virgin Island.

Essential St. Croix After-Golf Activities

  • Fine Dining: Award-winning restaurants with Caribbean and international cuisine
  • Beach Adventures: Pristine beaches perfect for relaxation and water sports
  • Cultural Tours: Historic sites and rum distillery experiences
  • Water Activities: Snorkeling, sailing, and underwater adventures
  • Local Experiences: Art galleries, markets, and authentic island culture

Fine Dining: Culinary Excellence After Championship Golf

St. Croix's dining scene rivals any Caribbean destination, offering everything from beachfront casual fare to sophisticated fine dining experiences. After a round at Carambola, treat yourself to the island's culinary treasures that showcase fresh local ingredients, Caribbean spices, and international influences.

St. Croix's award-winning restaurants offer the perfect setting for celebrating a great round at Carambola, featuring fresh Caribbean cuisine and waterfront ambiance.

Top Restaurant Recommendations

Savant Restaurant (Christiansted): This upscale establishment offers an innovative menu featuring fresh seafood and Caribbean-inspired dishes. Located in a historic 18th-century building, Savant provides an elegant atmosphere perfect for celebrating a great round of golf. Their lobster thermidor and locally caught mahi-mahi are consistently rated among the best on the island.

Rum Runners Steakhouse (Christiansted): For those craving premium steaks and fresh seafood, Rum Runners delivers exceptional quality in a sophisticated setting. Their extensive wine list and waterfront views make it ideal for romantic dinners or business entertainment after golf.

Kendricks Restaurant (Christiansted): Known for its French-Caribbean fusion cuisine, Kendricks offers an intimate dining experience with creative dishes that change seasonally. Their conch cakes and pan-seared grouper are local favorites.

Cheeseburgers in Paradise (Christiansted): For a more casual post-golf meal, this beachfront restaurant offers excellent burgers, fresh seafood, and tropical drinks with your toes in the sand. It's perfect for watching the sunset after an afternoon round.

Local Specialties to Try

  • Kallaloo: Traditional Caribbean soup with leafy greens and seafood
  • Johnny Cakes: Fried bread perfect with local fish
  • Conch Fritters: Island delicacy served with spicy dipping sauce
  • Local Fish: Mahi-mahi, grouper, and snapper prepared Caribbean-style
  • Rum Cocktails: Featuring locally distilled Cruzan Rum

Cultural Attractions: Discovering St. Croix's Rich Heritage

Beyond its natural beauty, St. Croix boasts a fascinating history spanning Danish colonial rule, sugar plantation era, and vibrant Caribbean culture. These cultural experiences provide perfect afternoon activities after morning golf rounds.

Explore historic Christiansted's Danish colonial architecture and Fort Christiansvaern, offering fascinating cultural experiences perfect for afternoon exploration after morning golf rounds at Carambola.

Historic Christiansted

Explore Christiansted National Historic Site, where Danish colonial architecture tells the story of St. Croix's sugar plantation era. Fort Christiansvaern, built in 1749, offers guided tours and stunning harbor views. The nearby Steeple Building houses fascinating exhibits about island history and culture.

Cruzan Rum Distillery

Visit the Cruzan Rum Distillery in Frederiksted for tours and tastings of premium Caribbean rum. Learn about the rum-making process while sampling aged varieties in a historic setting. The distillery's gift shop offers exclusive bottles perfect for taking home memories of your golf vacation.

Whim Plantation Museum

This restored sugar plantation provides insight into St. Croix's colonial past. The great house museum showcases period furniture and artifacts, while the grounds feature original windmill ruins and slave quarters that offer sobering reminders of the island's complex history.

Cultural Experience Tips

Many historic sites offer guided tours that provide deeper insights into St. Croix's multicultural heritage. Consider purchasing a heritage trail pass for discounted admission to multiple attractions. Learn more about island experiences on our St. Croix page.

Beach and Water Activities: Perfect Post-Golf Relaxation

St. Croix's pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters provide the perfect antidote to an exciting round of golf. Whether you prefer active water sports or peaceful beach relaxation, the island offers options for every preference.

St. Croix's pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters offer the perfect relaxation after challenging rounds at Carambola Golf Club, with world-class snorkeling and water sports.

Buck Island Reef National Monument

Just off St. Croix's northeast coast, Buck Island offers some of the Caribbean's best snorkeling and diving. Half-day tours with companies like Big Beard's Adventure Tours include boat transportation, snorkeling gear, and guided underwater trails through vibrant coral reefs teeming with tropical fish. Many tours include beach time on the island's pristine white sand beaches.

Cane Bay

Located on the island's north shore, Cane Bay is renowned for world-class diving and snorkeling directly from the beach. The famous Cane Bay Wall drops dramatically from shallow coral gardens to deep blue depths, making it accessible for both beginners and experienced divers. Cane Bay Dive Shop offers equipment rentals and guided tours.

Sandy Point Beach

This protected beach on the island's southwest tip offers excellent swimming and the chance to see endangered sea turtles during nesting season (March through October). The calm waters and soft sand make it perfect for post-golf relaxation.

Water Sports and Activities

  • Kayaking: Explore mangrove lagoons and secluded coves
  • Sailing: Charter boats for sunset cruises or day sails
  • Stand-up Paddleboarding: Perfect for calm bay areas
  • Deep-Sea Fishing: Charter boats for marlin, tuna, and mahi-mahi
  • Catamaran Tours: Multi-stop island hopping with snorkeling

Adventure Activities: Active Pursuits Beyond Golf

For those seeking more active pursuits after golf, St. Croix offers diverse adventure activities that showcase the island's natural beauty from different perspectives.

Explore St. Croix's natural beauty through hiking trails, botanical gardens, and adventure activities that provide the perfect complement to championship golf at Carambola.

Hiking and Nature Exploration

The island features several hiking trails through tropical forests and coastal areas. The Jack and Isaac Bay Preserve offers easy coastal walks with excellent bird watching, while the more challenging trails in the northwest mountains provide panoramic island views.

Sea Turtle Watching

St. Croix is a major nesting site for leatherback, hawksbill, and green sea turtles. Guided evening tours during nesting season offer the unforgettable experience of watching these magnificent creatures come ashore to lay eggs.

Botanical Gardens and Nature Tours

Visit St. George Village Botanical Garden, built on the ruins of a 19th-century plantation. The 17-acre garden showcases native Caribbean plants and provides peaceful walking paths perfect for recovering from a challenging round of golf.

Shopping and Local Crafts: Unique Island Treasures

St. Croix offers excellent shopping opportunities, from duty-free goods in Christiansted to local crafts and artwork that capture the island's creative spirit.

Discover unique island treasures and local crafts in Christiansted's historic shopping district, perfect for finding meaningful souvenirs from your Carambola golf vacation.

Christiansted Shopping District

The historic downtown area features duty-free shopping for jewelry, watches, and liquor, along with local boutiques selling Caribbean-made goods. Gallery shops showcase local artists' work, including paintings, pottery, and handcrafted jewelry.

Local Markets and Artisans

Visit weekend farmers' markets for fresh tropical fruits, local honey, and handmade crafts. Local artisans sell everything from woven baskets to hand-carved wooden sculptures that make perfect vacation souvenirs.

Pro Shopping Tips

  • Take advantage of duty-free shopping for significant savings on luxury goods
  • Look for locally made hot sauces and spice blends as unique gifts
  • Support local artists by purchasing original artwork and crafts
  • Many shops offer shipping services for larger purchases

Evening Entertainment: Nightlife and Cultural Events

As the sun sets over the Caribbean, St. Croix comes alive with evening entertainment options ranging from casual beach bars to sophisticated lounges and cultural performances.

Live Music and Bars

Experience local culture through music at venues featuring Caribbean bands, steel drum performances, and reggae groups. Many restaurants and bars offer live entertainment on specific nights, creating perfect opportunities to unwind with fellow golfers and locals.

Cultural Events and Festivals

Depending on your visit timing, you might experience local festivals, art walks, or cultural celebrations that showcase St. Croix's vibrant community spirit. Check the official USVI tourism website for current event calendars for concerts, art gallery openings, and seasonal celebrations.

Creating the Perfect Post-Golf Itinerary

To maximize your St. Croix experience, consider planning themed days that combine complementary activities. A cultural day might include historic Christiansted exploration followed by rum distillery tours and dinner at Savant Restaurant. An adventure day could feature morning snorkeling at Buck Island, afternoon beach time, and sunset dining at Cheeseburgers in Paradise.

For the ultimate relaxation day, start with a leisurely breakfast, enjoy spa services at your resort, explore local markets, and finish with waterfront dining as you watch the Caribbean sunset paint the sky in brilliant colors.

Frequently Asked Questions About St. Croix Activities

What are the best restaurants in St. Croix after golf?

Top restaurants include Savant Restaurant for upscale dining, Rum Runners Steakhouse for premium steaks, Kendricks for French-Caribbean fusion, and Cheeseburgers in Paradise for casual beachfront dining. All offer unique atmospheres perfect for post-golf celebrations.

What activities can I do in St. Croix besides golf?

Popular activities include snorkeling at Buck Island Reef National Monument, diving at Cane Bay Wall, visiting historic Christiansted, touring Cruzan Rum Distillery, exploring St. George Village Botanical Garden, and relaxing at Sandy Point Beach.

How do I get to Buck Island from St. Croix?

Buck Island tours depart from Christiansted harbor. Half-day tours include boat transportation, snorkeling gear, and guided underwater trails. Book with operators like Big Beard's Adventure Tours for the best experience.

What cultural attractions should I visit in St. Croix?

Must-visit cultural sites include Christiansted National Historic Site with Fort Christiansvaern, Whim Plantation Museum, Cruzan Rum Distillery, and St. George Village Botanical Garden for insights into the island's rich history.

Where can I go shopping in St. Croix?

Christiansted's historic downtown offers duty-free shopping for jewelry and luxury goods, local art galleries, and weekend farmers' markets featuring local crafts and produce. Many shops offer shipping services for convenient purchases.
