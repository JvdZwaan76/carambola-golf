The Ultimate Guide to Carambola Golf Resort: St. Croix's Premier Championship Course for 2025

Discover everything you need to know about Carambola Golf Club, from our legendary Robert Trent Jones Sr. design to insider tips for mastering our championship course and making the most of your Caribbean golf vacation.

Are you dreaming of trading your winter blues for turquoise seas, lush tropical valleys, and a round of golf that challenges your skills while soothing your soul? Look no further than Carambola Golf Resort in St. Croix, US Virgin Islands—a hidden gem where championship-level play meets Caribbean paradise.

Designed by the legendary Robert Trent Jones Sr., this 18-hole, par-72 course has earned rave reviews for its breathtaking views, strategic challenges, and year-round playability. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a casual enthusiast, Carambola stands out as one of the best golf courses in the US Virgin Islands, consistently ranked among the top in the region for its craftsmanship and scenic beauty.

A Legacy of Excellence: The History and Design of Carambola Golf Course St. Croix

Nestled on a former sugar plantation in the northwest hills of St. Croix, Carambola Golf Course opened in 1966 as part of a visionary development by Laurence Rockefeller. The course's architect, Robert Trent Jones Sr., called it one of his finest creations—and for good reason. Spanning 7,181 yards from the back tees, it weaves through undulating terrain, tropical foliage, and sparkling ponds, with the Caribbean Sea as your constant backdrop.

What sets Carambola apart? Its "pleasantly testing" layout combines strategic bunkers, water hazards on multiple holes, and elevation changes that demand precision and creativity. Recent upgrades, including new equipment and improved greens that roll smoothly and challenge even low-handicappers, have golfers buzzing.

Rated 4 stars by GOLF Magazine and praised on platforms like TripAdvisor for its "relaxing and enjoyable" vibe, the course often feels like your private oasis—deserted fairways mean no rush, just pure immersion in nature. Birdwatchers, bring binoculars: Pelicans, herons, and ducks frequent the ponds, adding to the serene, sustainable atmosphere we prioritize at Carambola.

Hole-by-Hole Highlights: What to Expect on the Course

Carambola Golf Course St. Croix isn't just about the views; it's a test of strategy and stamina. Here's a sneak peek at some standout holes:

Hole 1 (Par 4, 410 yards): The Welcome

A gentle opener downhill to a wide fairway, but beware the left-side bunker—set the tone with a solid drive.

Hole 7 (Par 3, 175 yards): The Signature

One of the signature holes, requiring a carry over water to an elevated green framed by palm trees. Recent reviews call it "challenging yet fair," with the sea breeze adding an extra layer of excitement.

Hole 12 (Par 5, 550 yards): The Risk-Reward

A risk-reward par 5 with uphill shots and cross-water hazards—go for the green in two if you're feeling bold, but the lush valley views might distract you!

Hole 18 (Par 4, 420 yards): The Grand Finale

Finish strong with a dogleg right, aiming for the clubhouse ruins in the distance. It's a fitting end to a round that's both exhilarating and relaxing.

Our Pro Shop offers PGA-certified instruction to sharpen your game, plus rentals for clubs and carts. Green fees are competitive: $110 for 18 holes with cart (walking $80), making it an affordable luxury compared to mainland courses. In 2025, we're introducing sustainable initiatives like eco-friendly maintenance to preserve St. Croix's natural beauty—play with a purpose!

Insider Tips for Your Best Round at Carambola Golf Resort

To maximize your experience:

Tee times fill up during peak season (December-April). Call our Pro Shop at +1-340-778-5638 or visit our site for reservations.

Weather Wisdom

St. Croix's tropical climate means year-round golf, but mornings are cooler—pack sunscreen and stay hydrated.

Gear Up

Our shop has everything from snacks to premium rentals. For lessons, our PGA pros focus on trade wind adjustments, a unique Caribbean challenge.

Group Perks

Hosting an event? Our packages include group rates and catering—ideal for weddings, retreats, or bachelor parties.

Recent Visitor Feedback

Pair Your Golf Getaway with St. Croix's Top Vacation Activities

Why stop at golf? Carambola Golf Resort is the perfect base for a full St. Croix adventure. Here's how to blend your rounds with island highlights:

Beach Bliss and Snorkeling

After golf, head to nearby beaches for world-class snorkeling—spot colorful reefs and sea turtles. Resorts like Carambola Beach Resort offer private shores and hot tubs for post-round relaxation. For more, explore our St. Croix page.

Tennis and More

Combine with tennis at spots like The Buccaneer Resort, or try disc golf and pickleball for variety.

Cultural Excursions

Explore Christiansted National Historic Site for historic sites, Cruzan Rum Distillery for rum tours, or hiking trails. For foodies, dine on fresh seafood at local eateries—many within a short drive.

Vacation Packages

Partner with nearby rentals or resorts for golf-inclusive stays, saving up to $1,000/week on self-catering villas near the course. Check options on our accommodations page.

St. Croix's 340 acres of oceanfront playground make it ideal for multi-activity trips—golf in the morning, beach vibes in the afternoon.

Frequently Asked Questions About Carambola Golf Resort

What makes Carambola the best golf course in St. Croix?

Carambola Golf Club features a Robert Trent Jones Sr. design with stunning Caribbean Sea views, strategic challenges, and sustainable features that set it apart for 2025 golf vacations. The course combines championship-level play with tropical beauty and year-round perfect weather.
