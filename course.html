A Robert Trent Jones Sr. Masterpiece

Carved from the natural contours of St. Croix's northwestern coastline, Carambola Golf Club stands as one of Robert Trent Jones Sr.'s most celebrated Caribbean designs. Each hole presents a unique challenge while showcasing the legendary architect's philosophy of strategic golf course design.

Jones' masterful routing takes advantage of the island's natural topography, creating dramatic elevation changes, strategic bunkering, and spectacular ocean vistas. The course rewards precise shot-making while offering multiple tee options to accommodate golfers of every skill level.

Design Excellence

Jones' signature elements create an unforgettable golf experience

Strategic Water Features

Jones masterfully incorporated natural and artificial water hazards that come into play on twelve holes, requiring precise shot placement and strategic course management throughout your round.

Dramatic Elevation Changes

The course features over 200 feet of elevation change, creating spectacular vistas and challenging lies that test every club in your bag while providing unforgettable golf experiences.

Championship Bunkering

Strategically placed bunkers reward accuracy and penalize wayward shots. Jones' signature deep bunkers and waste areas require both skill and course management to navigate successfully.

18 Holes of Championship Golf

Each hole at Carambola tells a unique story of Jones' design genius, offering distinct challenges while maintaining the seamless flow that defines championship golf. From dramatic ocean views to strategic water crossings, every hole provides a memorable experience in tropical paradise.
