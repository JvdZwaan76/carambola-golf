The Championship Course

18 holes of Robert Trent Jones Sr. masterpiece design in tropical paradise

A Jones Masterpiece

Robert Trent Jones Sr. created a championship layout that perfectly balances challenge with playability, incorporating St. Croix's natural beauty into every strategic decision. From elevated tees with stunning Caribbean vistas to greens protected by strategic bunkering, each hole tells its own story while contributing to an unforgettable round.

The course showcases Jones' legendary design philosophy: "Every hole should be a difficult par and a fair birdie." Trade winds, tropical vegetation, and dramatic elevation changes combine to create a truly unique Caribbean golf experience.

Walk in the Footsteps of Legends

Each hole was crafted with meticulous attention to strategy, beauty, and challenge. From dramatic elevation changes to strategic water hazards, every hole tells its own story while contributing to an unforgettable round of championship golf.
