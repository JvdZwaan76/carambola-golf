A Robert Trent Jones Sr. Masterpiece

Carved from the natural contours of St. Croix's northwestern coastline, Carambola Golf Club stands as one of Robert Trent Jones Sr.'s most celebrated Caribbean designs. Each hole presents a unique challenge while showcasing the legendary architect's philosophy of strategic golf course design.

Strategic Design Philosophy

Jones' masterful routing takes advantage of the island's natural topography, creating dramatic elevation changes, strategic bunkering, and spectacular ocean vistas. The course rewards precise shot-making while offering multiple tee options to accommodate golfers of every skill level.

Championship Golf in Paradise

From the opening tee shot to the dramatic finish at the 18th green, every hole at Carambola showcases the perfect marriage of challenging golf and breathtaking natural beauty that defines Caribbean golf at its finest.

Championship Course Design

Robert Trent Jones Sr.'s design philosophy is evident throughout Carambola's 18-hole championship layout. The master architect crafted each hole to challenge golfers while rewarding strategic thinking and precise execution.

The course winds through a lush tropical valley, taking advantage of natural elevation changes and incorporating the island's trade winds as a strategic element. From elevated tees to well-protected greens, every aspect of the design reflects Jones' legendary attention to both challenge and beauty.
