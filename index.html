A Legacy of Excellence

Welcome to Carambola Golf Club, where Robert Trent Jones Sr.'s masterful design meets the pristine beauty of St. Croix. Since opening, our championship course has challenged golfers with strategic bunkering, rolling fairways, and breathtaking Caribbean vistas.

Jones' signature style is evident throughout our 18-hole layout, featuring the perfect balance of challenge and playability that has made him golf's most celebrated architect. Every round at Carambola offers the opportunity to experience golf as it was meant to be played.

Championship Golf in Paradise

Where legendary design meets tropical beauty

Robert Trent Jones Sr. Design

Experience the work of golf's most celebrated architect. Jones' strategic design philosophy creates challenging yet fair golf that rewards precision and punishes wayward shots, all while incorporating the natural beauty of St. Croix.

Spectacular Island Setting

Rolling fairways wind through tropical landscape with stunning Caribbean Sea views. The course's elevation changes and natural contours create dramatic vistas and memorable golf experiences on every hole.

Championship Conditions

Pristine greens and immaculately maintained fairways ensure optimal playing conditions year-round. Our dedicated grounds crew maintains the course to championship standards, worthy of Jones' legendary design.

Experience St. Croix

Discover the island's finest dining and attractions beyond the fairways

Your championship golf experience at Carambola is just the beginning. St. Croix offers a wealth of world-class dining, pristine beaches, and cultural attractions that perfectly complement your tropical golf getaway. From farm-to-table cuisine to historic plantation tours, the island's sophisticated offerings rival any destination in the Caribbean.

Fine Dining

Ama at Cane Bay

Oceanfront fine dining at its Caribbean finest. This sustainable seafood restaurant offers tables positioned steps from the water's edge, where the sound of waves complements expertly prepared dishes using locally sourced ingredients. The menu changes seasonally, featuring fresh-caught fish and innovative preparations that showcase the island's natural bounty.

Cane Bay, North Shore
Sustainable Seafood
Modern American

Estera Kitchen & Bar

Located in historic downtown Christiansted, Estera elevates classic American flavors with Caribbean influences. The open kitchen design and intimate atmosphere create a sophisticated dining experience. Hand-crafted cocktails and an eclectic wine selection perfectly complement their innovative, uncategorized menu that changes with local availability.

Downtown Christiansted
Modern American
Farm to Table

Savant Restaurant

An eclectic culinary experience featuring daily fresh fish and lobster purchased directly from local fishermen. The farm-to-table philosophy incorporates spicy mixed greens, heirloom tomatoes, and aromatic herbs from island farmers. This Hospital Street gem combines healthy, fresh ingredients with creative preparations that celebrate local flavors.

Hospital Street, Christiansted
Farm to Table
French Caribbean

Too.Chez Restaurant & Bar

Housed in a stunning 1750s historic building, Too.Chez serves French-influenced dishes with American sophistication. The romantic courtyard setting provides an intimate atmosphere for enjoying specialties like baked brie with caramelized apples and house-made crostini. The wine selection and craft cocktails perfectly complement the refined menu.

Historic Downtown Christiansted
French Caribbean
Contemporary

Hamilton's Restaurant

Named for Alexander Hamilton and civil rights advocate David Hamilton Jackson, this upscale restaurant offers terrace dining with spectacular Christiansted Harbor views. The sophisticated menu celebrates both historical figures' legacy of public service while delivering exceptional contemporary cuisine in an elegant atmosphere.

Christiansted Harbor
Contemporary
Authentic Caribbean

Le Reine Chicken Shack

An island institution serving authentic Caribbean cuisine in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. The rotisserie and curry chicken are legendary, complemented by traditional sides like rice and beans, johnny cakes, and plantains. This local favorite offers genuine Crucian flavors in a casual, family-friendly setting.

Local Favorite
Authentic Caribbean
National Monument

Buck Island Reef National Monument

A pristine tropical paradise featuring one of the finest examples of living coral reef in the Caribbean. The underwater snorkeling trail offers guided exploration of diverse marine life, while Turtle Beach provides powder-soft sand and crystal-clear waters. This protected national monument represents the pinnacle of Caribbean natural beauty.

1.5 miles northeast of St. Croix
Snorkeling & Beach
Historic Landmark

Point Udall

The easternmost point of the United States, featuring the striking Millennium Monument sundial commemorating the year 2000. This dramatic hilltop location offers panoramic ocean views and marks the beginning of scenic hiking trails leading to secluded Isaac Bay. A perfect spot for sunrise photography and contemplation.

East End, St. Croix
Scenic Views & Hiking
Cultural Heritage

St. George Village Botanical Garden

A magnificent 16-acre botanical wonderland built among the ruins of a 19th-century sugar plantation. The garden showcases tropical plants, heritage buildings, and archaeological remains dating back to 100 A.D. Winding paths lead through themed sections highlighting both natural beauty and cultural history.

Centerline Road, St. Croix
Botanical Gardens
Natural Wonder

Salt River Bay

A National Historic Park and Ecological Preserve featuring mangrove-lined waters perfect for kayaking and one of the world's rare bioluminescent bays. Evening tours reveal the magical glow of microscopic organisms while daytime excursions explore the rich ecosystem where Columbus first landed in 1493.

North Shore, St. Croix
Kayaking & Bioluminescence
Wildlife Preserve

Sandy Point National Wildlife Refuge

The longest stretch of beach in the US Virgin Islands, this pristine three-mile sanctuary protects endangered leatherback turtle nesting sites. Open seasonally, the refuge offers unspoiled Caribbean beauty with minimal development, making it perfect for nature lovers seeking pristine coastal experiences.

Southwest Point, St. Croix
Wildlife & Beach
Historical Museum

Estate Whim Plantation Museum

The only sugar plantation museum in the Virgin Islands, set on magnificently landscaped grounds. The restored 18th-century windmill and great house provide insight into St. Croix's sugar plantation era. Interactive exhibits detail the complex history of sugar production and its impact on Caribbean society.

Centerline Road, St. Croix
Historical Museum
World-Class Diving

Cane Bay Wall Diving

One of the Caribbean's premier dive sites, featuring a dramatic underwater wall that plunges thousands of feet into the deep blue. The shallow reef offers excellent snorkeling while experienced divers can explore the wall's depths, encountering sea turtles, rays, and an abundance of tropical fish.

Cane Bay, North Shore
Diving & Snorkeling
Adventure Tours

Horseback Riding Adventures

Explore St. Croix's pristine beaches and tropical landscapes on horseback with guided tours along white sand shores. These memorable excursions combine the romance of horseback riding with the beauty of Caribbean beaches, creating an unforgettable island adventure perfect for all skill levels.

Cowboy Beach, West End
Horseback Tours
Cultural Experience

Rum Distillery Tours

Discover the art of rum production at St. Croix's world-renowned distilleries. Learn about the distillation process, sugar cane cultivation, and the island's rum heritage while sampling premium spirits. These educational tours provide insight into one of the Caribbean's most important cultural traditions.

Various Locations
Rum Tasting Tours
Water Sports

Sailing & Sunset Cruises

Experience the Caribbean's turquoise waters aboard luxury sailing vessels. Half-day and full-day charters offer snorkeling, swimming, and relaxation, while sunset cruises provide romantic endings to perfect days. Professional crews ensure safe, comfortable exploration of St. Croix's stunning coastline.

Christiansted & Frederiksted
Sailing Charters
Historic Walking

Historic Christiansted Walking Tour

Explore the colorful colonial architecture and rich history of Christiansted's National Historic Site. Cobblestone streets lead past Danish colonial buildings, historic forts, and the former home of Alexander Hamilton. This self-guided or guided tour reveals centuries of Caribbean history.

Downtown Christiansted
Historic Walking Tour
Sport Fishing

Deep Sea Fishing Charters

St. Croix's unique location at the edge of the continental shelf provides exceptional deep-sea fishing just two miles offshore. Target marlin, mahi-mahi, tuna, and wahoo with experienced captains who know the waters. Half-day and full-day charters accommodate all skill levels.
