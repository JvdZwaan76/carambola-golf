About This Website

The Carambola Golf Club website showcases our championship 18-hole golf course designed by Robert Trent Jones Sr. in St. Croix, US Virgin Islands. Our site provides comprehensive information about golf rates, tee time bookings, luxury accommodations, fine dining, and island attractions to help you plan the perfect Caribbean golf vacation.

All content is regularly updated to ensure accuracy and relevance. For the most current information about course conditions, rates, or availability, please contact our Pro Shop directly.
