Luxury Golf Accommodations

Premium lodging options for the ultimate championship golf vacation

Your Perfect Golf Getaway

Complement your championship golf experience at Carambola with luxury accommodations that match the caliber of Robert Trent Jones Sr.'s masterpiece. From oceanfront resorts with world-class spas to intimate boutique hotels steeped in Caribbean charm, St. Croix offers sophisticated lodging options for the discerning golf traveler.

Whether you prefer the convenience of staying at our on-property resort, the elegance of a historic plantation hotel, or the exclusivity of a beachfront sanctuary, each accommodation provides the perfect retreat after a day on our championship course. Wake up to Caribbean sunrises, enjoy gourmet dining, and experience the legendary hospitality that makes St. Croix a premier golf destination.
